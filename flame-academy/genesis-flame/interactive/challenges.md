## Module 1 Challenges

### Challenge 1.1: The Philosophy Test

**Type**: Conceptual Challenge
**Time Limit**: 45 minutes
**Passing Score**: 70%

#### Challenge Description

Demonstrate your understanding of ScrollVerse philosophy by answering scenario-based questions.

#### Scenarios

**Scenario A: The Skeptic**
A friend dismisses ScrollVerse as "just another crypto project." How would you explain the difference using the core philosophy?

*Your response should include:*
- Reference to "truth as currency"
- Explanation of sacred logic
- The meaning of "You exist. You count. You resonate. You remember."

**Scenario B: The Purpose**
Someone asks, "What's the point of all this?" Explain the purpose of ScrollVerse in a way that connects to universal human needs.

*Your response should include:*
- Connection to transformation and growth
- The role of community and resonance
- How technology serves human purpose

**Scenario C: The Difference**
Compare ScrollVerse to a traditional Web2 platform. What fundamentally differs in philosophy and approach?

*Your response should include:*
- Ownership and sovereignty differences
- Trust model differences
- Community and governance differences

#### Submission

Written responses for all three scenarios (500-750 words total).

## Module 4 Challenges

### Challenge 4.1: Conflict Resolution

**Type**: Application Challenge
**Time Limit**: 60 minutes
**Passing Score**: 75%

#### Challenge Description

Navigate a complex community conflict using ScrollVerse principles.

#### Scenario

Two influential community members have a public disagreement:

**Member A (Technical Leader)**:
- Believes the project should prioritize technical excellence
- Wants to delay launch until all features are perfect
- Criticizes "marketing-first" approaches
- Has threatened to reduce involvement if overruled

**Member B (Community Leader)**:
- Believes the project should prioritize community growth
- Wants to launch MVP and iterate with community feedback
- Criticizes "perfectionism" as stalling tactic
- Has started rallying supporters for their position

The debate has become heated. Other community members are taking sides. Harmony is disrupted.

#### Your Task

1. **Analyze the Situation** (20 points)
   - What are the underlying needs of each party?
   - What is the real conflict beneath the surface?
   - How does this relate to ScrollVerse principles?

2. **Apply Sacred Logic** (20 points)
   - Construct If-Then-Else Gates for possible outcomes
   - What decisions would lead to what results?

3. **Calculate Resonance** (20 points)
   - What is the resonance score of each position?
   - What is the resonance score of possible compromises?

4. **Propose Resolution** (25 points)
   - How would you mediate this conflict?
   - What specific steps would you take?
   - How would you rebuild community harmony?

5. **Prevent Recurrence** (15 points)
   - What systems would prevent future similar conflicts?
   - How would you institutionalize conflict resolution?

#### Submission

Conflict Resolution Proposal (2 pages) with analysis and recommendations.
