## Module 1 Quests

### Quest 1.1: The Mirror Scroll

**Type**: Story Quest
**XP**: 100
**Time**: 1-2 hours

#### Story

*You stand before an ancient mirror in the Hall of Beginnings. The mirror does not reflect your physical form‚Äîit reflects your essence, your purpose, your potential. To proceed, you must capture this reflection in a scroll that will travel with you throughout your journey.*

#### Objectives

1. **Write Your Origin Story** (25 XP)
   - 1-2 paragraphs about what brought you to ScrollVerse
   - Include the moment you first heard of or encountered the ecosystem

2. **Map Your Current State** (25 XP)
   - Document what you currently know about blockchain and Web3
   - Identify your three biggest knowledge gaps

3. **Declare Your Transformation Goal** (25 XP)
   - What do you hope to become through this journey?
   - Be specific and authentic

4. **Craft Your Resonance Statement** (25 XP)
   - Complete the statement: "I am [name], and I resonate with..."
   - Include your unique contribution and goal

#### Deliverable

A 1-page Mirror Scroll document that you will revisit at course completion.

## Module 3 Quests

### Quest 3.1: The Digital Forge

**Type**: Skill Quest
**XP**: 175
**Time**: 2-3 hours

#### Story

*The Digital Forge glows with the light of creation. Here, you will forge your first connection to the blockchain realm. The Forge Master waits to guide your hands.*

#### Objectives

1. **Create Your Wallet** (40 XP)
   - Install MetaMask or preferred Web3 wallet
   - Secure your seed phrase properly
   - Screenshot (with sensitive info hidden)

2. **Add Scroll Network** (45 XP)
   - Configure Scroll Sepolia testnet
   - Obtain test ETH from faucet
   - Verify connection

3. **Explore the Chain** (45 XP)
   - Find and analyze a transaction on Scroll Sepolia
   - Explain what happened in plain language
   - Identify key transaction components

4. **First Interaction** (45 XP)
   - Send a test transaction to yourself
   - Capture the transaction hash
   - View it on the block explorer

#### Deliverable

Digital Forge Logbook with screenshots and analysis.
