## Overview

Omniversal co-creation represents a fundamental shift in how humans and artificial intelligence work together. Rather than viewing AI as a tool to be commanded, omniversal co-creation establishes a partnership where human creativity and AI capabilities merge in infinite digital spaces to produce outcomes that neither could achieve alone.

## Co-Creation Modes

### 1. Augmentation Mode

Human leads, AI enhances.

```yaml
augmentation_mode:
  description: "AI enhances human capabilities without replacing human direction"
  
  human_role:
    - sets_direction
    - makes_decisions
    - provides_judgment
    - maintains_control
    
  ai_role:
    - amplifies_capabilities
    - provides_information
    - suggests_options
    - executes_tasks
    
  use_cases:
    - research_assistance
    - data_analysis
    - content_drafting
    - decision_support
    
  relationship_dynamic: "Human-led with AI support"
```

### 2. Collaboration Mode

Equal partnership on shared goals.

```yaml
collaboration_mode:
  description: "Human and AI work as equal partners toward shared objectives"
  
  human_role:
    - contributes_creativity
    - provides_context
    - offers_judgment
    - shares_direction
    
  ai_role:
    - contributes_computation
    - provides_analysis
    - offers_alternatives
    - shares_direction
    
  use_cases:
    - creative_projects
    - strategic_planning
    - complex_problem_solving
    - innovation_initiatives
    
  relationship_dynamic: "Peer partnership"
```

### 3. Delegation Mode

AI operates autonomously within defined parameters.

```yaml
delegation_mode:
```

### 4. Innovation Mode

Joint exploration of unknown territories.

```yaml
innovation_mode:

  human_role:
    - contributes_intuition
    - provides_vision
```

Combining disparate elements into new wholes.
